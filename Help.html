Getting Started

BlockBuddy is a web application that helps you connect with your community by helping one another though posting and accepting requests. Get started by creating your account!

  • Click on 'Login/Sign Up' by the top right corner of the page.
  • Enter your Email, Name, and Password.
  • Once logged in, you can create new requests, view current requests, and also view requests you've accepted.
  • Click on 'My Profile' to edit your personal details or location.

Creating Requests

To create a new request:

  • Click on 'Post Requests' by the top right corner of the page.
  • Fill in the New Request form, and select the appropriate parameters for 'Paid' or 'Urgent'.
  • Select your location for the request.
  • Click on the 'Post Request' button to finalize your Request!

Viewing Requests

To view active Requests:

  • Click on 'View Requests' by the top right corner of the page.
  • Browse through the active Requests.
  • Click on 'Read More/Reply' on the desired Request.
  • Type your question or response in the Details box.

Need Help?

If you have any questions or need further assistance with BlockBuddy, please don't hesitate to contact our support team at support@blockbuddy.com
